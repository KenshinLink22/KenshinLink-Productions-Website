KenshinLink Productions, formerly Last Bosses Films, is a Stop Motion YouTube channel created by Angel Hernandez Vega on April 23, 2017.

Stop motion animation is the creation of videos by taking images with slight differences between each frame and stringing them together to form a video.

The purpose of the channel is to express my passion for stop motion filming and have fun with a hobby that I can share with others. Uploads are yearly, as stop motion takes time, especially vfx editing. I like to invest little into theses videos, as they are only a hobby; though, I do upgrade to better equipment and resources.
